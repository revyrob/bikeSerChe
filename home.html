AUDIO THEATRE EXPERIENCE

Let us take you through the small streets and passages to discover the history and legends of Briançon.

You will be entertained by stories of the Celts who settled in this region 2000 years ago, the fires that burnt down the town of Briançon twice, witches, and much more! There is added music and sounds to create an ambiance you won't forget.

When you purchase the audio from our site you will receive the audio, a map, photos, and street names for each stop. Follow the map from Chapter 1 to Chapter 9. You will need a smartphone, headphones, and WiFi (to download the audio).

The audio theatre experience takes between 60-90 minutes to complete and we recommend to do it in the late afternoon or evening because the atmosphere in the old town of Briançon is mystical as dusk arrives.

By purchasing this audio theatre experience, you agree to the following: if you injure yourself, pass out, lose your false teeth, contract the plague, your hair turns white with dread or anything that could harm your health - will be your fault! The audio theatre experience is not recommend for children under 8 years of age.

COST

By pressing PURCHASE you will be redirected to Paypal and you can purchase the audio for €3.50. When you purchase the audio a URL will be included with the payment confirmation.

**You need to open the audio URL in a Chrome Browser for an Android or in Safari for Apple.**

If you have 4G you can listen to the audio direct from the audio URL. Or you can download the audio by pressing on the 3 buttons on the side of the audio player and download the audio. If you have any problems email us.

The route map, photos and street names are included in the audio URL.

**You have access to the audio URL for 24hrs from your purchase.**
