Briançon Fantômes

AUDIO THEATRE EXPERIENCE

Join us in an exciting way to discover the history and legends of Briançon. Briançon Fantômes is an audio theatre experience where you walk around the old town of Briançon and are entertained with stories relating to the old town. All you need is your smartphone and a pair of headphones. The audio experience and walk takes between 60-90 minutes to complete and we recommend to do it in the late afternoon or evening.
Let us take you through the small streets and passages to discover what is hidden inside. By purchasing this adventure, you agree to the following: if you injure yourself, pass out, lose your false teeth, contract the plague, your hair turns white with dread or anything that could harm your health - will be your fault. The audio theatre experience is not recommend for small children.
