AUDIO THEATRE EXPERIENCE

Let us take you through the small streets and passages to discover the history and legends of Briançon. Briançon Fantômes is an audio theatre experience. Purchase the audio from our site, then follow our map through the old town of Briançon. You will need a smartphone, headphones, and WiFi (to download the audio). The audio theatre experience takes between 60-90 minutes to complete and we recommend to do it in the late afternoon or evening because the atmosphere in the old town of Briançon is mystical as dusk arrives.
By purchasing this audio theatre experience, you agree to the following: if you injure yourself, pass out, lose your false teeth, contract the plague, your hair turns white with dread or anything that could harm your health - will be your fault. The audio theatre experience is not recommend for children under 8 years of age.

COST

By pressing PURCHASE you will be redirected to Paypal and you can purchase the audio for €3.50. When you purchase the audio a URL will be included with the payment confirmation. The URL will direct you to a secure file transfer website where you can download the audio to your phone. The route map is found on our website under 'The Route' with photos refering to each stop. You will have access to the URL for 24hrs from your purchase.

TERMS OF PURCHASE

By purchasing this audio theatre experience you agree not to share the audio. All purchases are final.
